Riley Andrews

Therapist, MA, LMHC

Restoring Wellness was created at a point in my life when I needed more for myself and my family. As the mama of two young kiddos, who is also coping with life’s transitions, I’ve made it my mission to help mamas thrive throughout their pregnancy and postpartum phase(s) of life, ensuring a healthy and fulfilling lifestyle for years to come.

After working with kids for most of my career, I had my “ahh ha” moment during my first maternity leave. I thought, “if I can help mamas manage their mental health and wellbeing, I can prevent kiddos from ever needing to come to see me.” So, with that thought, I became fully trained to work with perinatal mental health through Postpartum Support International.

Being a mama is hard. Being a mama who raises emotionally intelligent kids can feel impossible. I will guide you to be at your best so that you can raise your baby/babies to be their strongest selves. It is always an honor to work with a mama who recognizes the importance of filling their cup, to be able to fill the cup of their little(s). You can reach me at
