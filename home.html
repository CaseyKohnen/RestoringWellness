Restoring Wellness is a mental health group practice that aims to engage the mind, nurture the spirit, and promote wholeness within Iowa and the greater Tri-States Area.

Riley Andrews

Mental Health Therapist, LMHC
Area of Focus: Perinatal, Postpartum, and Mood Disorders

Riley is a licensed Mental Health Counselor and has been trained through Postpartum Support International, specializing in Perinatal Mental Health and Mood Disorders. Riley also has training in grief, Chrisitan Counseling, and trauma through The Body Keeps Score, EMDR, Brainspotting, and Emotional Freedom Techniques.

Chloe Heberlein

Mental Health Therapist, tLMHC
Area of Focus: Life Adjustment, Relational Work, and Grief

Chloe utilizes a humanistic approach to honor the uniqueness of each individual and their circumstances. Chloe utilizes a variety of modalities to aid in creating genuine connections to support transforming relationships and boundaries within personal relationships. Chloe specifically utilizes techniques from: Dialectical Behavioral Therapy, Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy, Brainspotting, and Emotion-Focused Therapy.

Brandee Kohnen

Mental Health Therapist, LISW
Area of Focus: Eating Disorders, Anxiety, and Body Image

Brandee creates a non judgemental environment to support each client in feeling confident and comfortable in working toward their unique, individual goals. She meets the client where they are at to support them in regaining control of their brain health so that it no longer interferes with achieving the life goals that are important to them. Brandee utilizes techniques from: Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, and Strengths Based Approaches
